Danipedia
Ruby/Rails, Postgres, HTML, CSS

This project is a message board site created with a postgreSQL database using Ruby on Rails. The idea for the app originated with my dad, who passed away this year unexpectedly. I wanted a place for me and my siblings to be able to post memories and celebrate all of the good times we had with my father. His name, 'Dan', also served as inspiration for the name of the site. Feel free to play around with my app more
here.
